Copycat chipotle Chicken


Ingredients

  • 1 ounce dried chipotle chile pepper
  • 1 ounce dried ancho chile pepper
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ red onion, cut into small chunks
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Place chipotle and ancho chile peppers in a shallow bowl; pour in water.
Cover the bowl and let sit at room temperature until peppers are softened, 10 to 12 hours.
Drain water and remove seeds from peppers.

  2. Step 2

    3. Blend chile peppers, red onion, garlic, sea salt, cumin, oregano, and black pepper in a blender until a coarse paste forms;
add olive oil and blend until marinade is smooth.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place chicken thighs between 2 sheets of heavy plastic on a solid, level surface.
Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a 1/2-inch thickness.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag and add marinade.
Turn chicken several times to coat. Seal bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.

  5. Step 5

    6. Preheat an indoor grill with top and bottom plates for medium-high heat.

  6. Step 6

    7. Remove chicken from the bag and discard marinade.

  7. Step 7

    8. Place chicken on the preheated grill, close the lid, and cook until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear,
5 to 7 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
Cut chicken into strips and serve warm.
